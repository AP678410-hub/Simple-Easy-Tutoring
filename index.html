About Us

We provide tailored, high-quality education programs for students in Years 1 to 6. Our passionate Year 7 tutors foster curiosity, confidence, and academic growth through engaging lessons and interactive support.

Each session begins with a short review of what was covered in the previous lesson, helping reinforce learning and identify areas that need more attention.

Payment: $15/hour for 1-on-1 sessions, $10/hour for group classes. (Per Person)

Subscriptions: 1 Month – $125 | 1 Year – $600

Please note: Educational materials and textbooks are not provided as part of our tuition service. We kindly ask parents to acquire any necessary textbooks independently to support their child’s learning.

STRIVE Program

S.T.R.I.V.E. represents our core commitment to student success: Support, Targeted Learning, Resilience, Improvement, Vision, and Encouragement. This program is thoughtfully designed to empower students who may be experiencing academic challenges by providing consistent, focused instruction across all core subjects on a weekly basis. Through a nurturing environment and personalized support, STRIVE helps students build lasting confidence, strengthen essential skills, and develop a positive mindset towards learning.

Cost: $75 per week

Students enrolled in STRIVE benefit from small group settings, personalized progress tracking, and motivational workshops designed to foster a growth mindset.

Strive Referral Program

At Simple & Easy Tutoring, we appreciate your support and trust. Our Strive Referral Program rewards you for helping us grow our community of learners!

How It Works

  • Refer a Friend: Help others discover the value of quality education, share the advantages of our personalised tutoring services with friends and family, and let them experience the difference exceptional support can make in a student’s learning journey.
  • Your Reward: Enjoy a 15% discount on your next tutoring session for every successful referral.
  • Friend’s Reward: Your referred friend also receives 10% off their first tutoring session.
  • No Free Sessions: Please note, free sessions are not part of the referral rewards.

Additional Benefits

  • Stack Discounts: You can accumulate discounts from multiple referrals and apply them to future sessions.
  • Exclusive Events: Access to special workshops and group activities reserved for our valued Strive Program members.
  • Priority Booking: Early access to new session times and limited-availability programs.

To participate, simply ask your referred friend to mention your name during their sign-up. Discounts will be applied automatically to your next session invoice.
