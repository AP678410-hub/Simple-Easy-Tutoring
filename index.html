About Us

At Simple & Easy Tutoring, we deliver personalized, high-quality educational support for students in Years 1 through 6. Our dedicated Year 7 tutors are committed to fostering curiosity, confidence, and academic excellence through engaging, interactive lessons tailored to each student’s needs.

Each session begins with a concise review of previous material to reinforce learning and address any challenges, ensuring steady progress.

Rates: $15 per hour for one-on-one tutoring; $10 per hour for group sessions (per student).

Subscriptions: Monthly plan - $125 | Annual plan - $600

Please note: Educational resources and textbooks are not included. We recommend parents procure any necessary materials to support their child’s learning journey.

Subjects Offered

  • English: Grammar, comprehension, punctuation, writing, and vocabulary development.
  • Mathematics: Fundamental concepts, problem-solving, mental arithmetic, and number fluency.
  • Science: Inquiry-based learning, hands-on STEM experiments, and scientific literacy.
  • Humanities & Social Sciences: Australian history, geography, civics, and economics.
  • Technology & STEM: Coding, robotics, digital literacy, and design thinking.

Programs by Age Group

  • Years 1–3: Led by Jack Kinnane and Arjun Patkar, focusing on foundational literacy, numeracy, and social-emotional skills development.
  • Years 4–6: Led by Mikael Zahid and Zahin Raki, emphasizing advanced concepts, critical thinking, and integrated STEM learning.
