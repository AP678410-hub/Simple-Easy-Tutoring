About Us

We provide tailored, high-quality education programs for students in Years 1 to 6. Our passionate Year 7 tutors foster curiosity, confidence, and academic growth through engaging lessons and interactive support.

Each session begins with a short review of what was covered in the previous lesson, helping reinforce learning and identify areas that need more attention.

Payment: $15/hour for 1-on-1 sessions, $10/hour for group classes. (Per Person)

Subscriptions: 1 Month – $125 | 1 Year – $600

Please note: Educational materials and textbooks are not provided as part of our tuition service. We kindly ask parents to acquire any necessary textbooks independently to support their child’s learning.
